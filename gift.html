Thank You, Joya

❤❤❤
Some people walk into your life quietly, without a grand entrance or warning, and somehow end up becoming the most comforting part of your day. You're that person for me.
We've known each other longer than we even realized, same school, same corridors, same air, but it took years for our stories to finally meet. Now, it's hard to imagine a day where I don't check my phone hoping to see your name.
There's something so grounding about you. You don't pretend. You don't try too hard. You're just real in a way that most people have forgotten how to be.
You can be thoughtful and unpredictable, silent and expressive, calm and clever, all at once and somehow, it never feels confusing. It just feels like... you.
I never really understood why your messages made me smile so easily, or why your quiet days felt peaceful instead of empty. It's only when I look back that I realize how much I noticed, even without meaning to. Like the way your eyes soften when you smile, Your cheeks have this soft fullness, the kind that makes your smile feel like it's resting on clouds.
Something I noticed your cheeks are a little fuller than your head shape, just enough to give this soft, pear-like shape. They lift slightly when you smile, and I end up noticing them every single time.
Your front teeth show up first when you smile. You said they’re normal and they are. But I noticed them once, quietly, and for some reason, that was enough. I used to think I never noticed people that closely but thinking about you, I realized I do or maybe I just noticed you without realizing I was.
You've become one of the very first people I run to with my little achievements. I tell you about all the tiny things that happened, everyone and everything that annoyed me, like a little kid complaining. And yes, a little kid because with you, I can say I'm awesome, I can say I'm immortal, I can say I'd win a fight with a grizzly bear. I can say I'm Batman, I'm Spiderman, I'm a dinosaur whatever my 11-year-old self once told my mom, my 18-year-old self now tells you. Thank you for keeping my inner child alive. I can totally be a kid around you. I can totally be stupid, totally show you my weaker side the side people sometimes call "girly" and still not feel weird about it.
You've given me the space to be fully myself to say the most random things, to be silly, bold, soft without ever making me feel small for it. That means more to me than I can explain.
Sometimes I just sit inside your chat even when there's nothing left to say. I don't really mind the silence. Somehow, even that feels like talking.
Thank you for all the things you do without knowing you're doing them for listening even when I ramble, for letting me be a little dramatic, and for showing up in ways I didn't know I needed.
This isn't just a thank-you for a few good conversations. It's for the comfort, the honesty, the space, and the sense of home that you somehow manage to carry with you.
Happy birthday, Joya. I hope you never lose whatever it is that makes you you. Because honestly, that's the part I like the most.
❤❤❤

-Awesome Adithyan
